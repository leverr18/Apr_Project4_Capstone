Welcome to the Uber & Lyft Rideshare Analysis

Explore, analyze, and gain insights into the rideshare industry through our detailed dashboards and analysis of Uber & Lyft data.

Project Overview

This project dives deep into the rideshare industry by comparing key metrics and trends between Uber and Lyft. Explore our dashboards and analysis sections for detailed insights.

What You Can Explore

  • Home – A brief overview of the project and its goals.
  • Tableau 1 – Visualizing Uber vs. Lyft ridership, pricing trends, and growth over time.
  • Tableau 2 – A deep dive into the geographical distribution of rides and key performance metrics.
  • Analysis – In-depth analysis of trends, such as demand patterns, time-of-day performance, and service efficiency.
  • Sources – A list of data sources and references used in the project to collect and analyze the rideshare data.
  • About Us – Information about the team behind the project and our mission.

Why Uber & Lyft?

The Uber and Lyft ridesharing services have transformed urban transportation, offering fast and affordable options for users. By comparing the two services, we aim to uncover interesting patterns and insights that could help both companies improve their services and provide users with better transportation options.
